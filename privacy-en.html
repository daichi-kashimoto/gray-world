Privacy Policy

Gray World (“the App”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what data may be processed, for what purposes, with whom it may be shared, and your rights.

1. Information We Process

  • App core: No account is required. We do not intentionally collect information that directly identifies you.
  • Advertising (Google AdMob): To serve and measure ads, the AdMob SDK may process device-related data (e.g., device identifiers, IP address, ad-serving metadata). Depending on region and consent status, ads may be personalized or non-personalized.
  • Analytics/Diagnostics: Anonymous crash or usage data may be used to improve quality.

2. Purposes of Use

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App
  • Serve and measure ads (subject to consent where required)
  • Troubleshooting and security

3. Sharing with Third Parties

We use third-party services such as Google AdMob. These providers process data under their own privacy policies.

  • Google’s EU user consent policy overview: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/10114014

4. Regional Requirements

  • EEA/UK (GDPR): We obtain consent for ads and cookies where required, using a Google-certified CMP. You can withdraw consent at any time.
  • US State Laws: We provide opt-out options where applicable (e.g., “Do Not Sell/Share”).
  • Children: The App is not directed to children under 13.

5. Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for the stated purposes or as required by law. Third-party retention follows each provider’s policy.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or withdraw consent. Contact us to exercise your rights.

7. Security

We implement appropriate safeguards to protect data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
